# Disclaimer

## Not a Medical Device

This repository contains a **demonstration workflow** for educational and portfolio purposes only.

It is **NOT**:
- A medical device under EU MDR 2017/745
- A certified AI system under EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- Validated for clinical use with real patient data
- Intended to replace clinical judgment or professional medical advice

## Synthetic Data Only

All clinical documents in `sample-data/` contain **entirely synthetic, fictitious information**. No real Protected Health Information (PHI) or personal data is included. All patient names, identification numbers, clinical values, and dates are invented.

## Not Clinically Validated

This software has **NOT** undergone:
- Clinical safety assessment (ISO 14971 risk management)
- Conformity assessment under EU AI Act (Regulation 2024/1689)
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) under GDPR
- Validation against clinical coding standards (ICD-10, LOINC, SNOMED CT)
- Testing with real clinical workflows or Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems

## Usage Warning

**DO NOT** deploy this workflow in a clinical environment without:
- Institutional review and approval
- Clinical validation and testing
- Regulatory compliance assessment
- Data Protection Impact Assessment
- Appropriate clinical safety governance (e.g., DCB0129 or equivalent)
- Human-in-the-loop oversight for all clinical decisions
